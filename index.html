THE TRUTH

BEHIND CARDSPLUG

Our personalised cards tell the story of passionate football fans looking to celebrate football in a unique way.

But truth be told, they’re not only limited to “fans”. We started off because we saw many idolised footballers show off their own personalised football cards, such as Messi, Ronaldo, Hazard & many more.

Unfortunately, at that time, the cards were only available to them and that didn't sit well with us, so we decided to create Cardsplug. A way for you, a true football fan, to be able to customise your own personalised card and relate directly to those top footballers.
